Privacy Policy for UIAList

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how UIAList ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our UI Automation Control Browser application ("the Service").

UIAList is designed as a privacy-first accessibility tool that operates entirely on your local device without collecting, transmitting, or storing any personal data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

UIAList does not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Usage analytics or telemetry data
  • UI control information or application data accessed through the tool
  • Keyboard inputs, mouse actions, or interaction patterns
  • Screen reader information or accessibility settings
  • Device information, system configurations, or hardware details
  • Network activity or internet browsing data
  • Location data
  • Crash reports or error logs

3. Local Operation

UIAList operates entirely on your local Windows device using the Microsoft UI Automation framework. All functionality is contained within your device, and no data leaves your computer.

4. No Internet Connection Required

UIAList does not require an internet connection to function. The application does not communicate with external servers, analytics services, or third-party platforms.

5. Third-Party Services

UIAList does not integrate with or use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms that could access your data.

6. Data Security

Since UIAList does not collect or transmit any data, there are no data security concerns related to external data handling. All operations remain within your local system's security boundary.

7. Children's Privacy

UIAList does not collect any information from users of any age. Our application is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

9. Open Source

UIAList is open source software. You can review our complete source code at https://github.com/slohmaier/UIAList to verify our privacy practices.

11. Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and Microsoft Store requirements. Since no personal data is collected, processed, or stored, most data protection obligations do not apply to UIAList.

This privacy policy reflects UIAList's commitment to user privacy and accessibility without compromising on functionality or user experience.
